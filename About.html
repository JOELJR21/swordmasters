What is Sword Masters?

Sword Masters is a video game series proposed by Joel Cruz Jr, the chief director of Healios Game Studios. It was once a one-off game idea, but as he grew older, Mr. Cruz cultivated a foundation for this series.


How many entries does it have?

As of now, Sword Masters has 19 main-stay entries, and 20 special entries. It begins from Dawn of Beginnings and the series finale ends with Final Revelation.



Characters

All characters have been given names. Approximately 200 characters are in this series. Each of them are individually different and have their own weapon style or elemental alignment.


Modes

Here are some of the modes that will be in this series:

  • Story Mode- Go through the central story of Sword Masters
  • Creation Mode- Create your own Sword Master
  • Chronicle Mode- Play any character's story
  • Pilgrimage Mode- Take on special missions and earn rewards and other goodies
  • Theatre- View cutscenes
  • Studio Mode- The game's sound test and gallery
  • Blacksmith- Buy new weapons or create your own
  • Shop- Purchase new items such as clothing, elemental attributes, and other items for customizing your Sword Master
